Momentum is a method that helps accelerate SGD in the relevant direction and dampens oscillations. It does this by adding a fraction beta of the update vector of the past time step to the current update vector.

Algorithm

1. Pick a starting value.
2. Pick number of iterations.
3. Calculate the new point while xnew - xold > precision.

        Vi := beta * Vi-1 + (1 - beta) * df/dxold           xnew := xold - alpha *Vi

Hyperparameters

The momentum term beta is usually set to 0.9 or a similiar value.
